Carpé Data!

A WebBox Prototype Usability Study

Do you use Facebook, Google Plus, or Linked-In? How about Nike+, Nike Fuel, Quicken Online or Apple's iCloud? Most people provide these services with a vast quantity of information about themselves to share with their friends. What if you, yourself, could make use of all this data for performing routine tasks? Like planning a party, for example, or keeping better track of your finances?

We're designing an interface we call the Data Palette which lets people pull together information from various sources and mix them to do routine tasks.

We need your help!

We need people to try out an early prototype on simulated tasks with synthesized data (we won't ask you to use your Facebook account or to disclose any of your online data). You need to come to Building 32 on the Highfield Campus some time before 10 September in order to participate. It should take 20-30 minutes to complete. We will be happy to compensate you with a £5 Sainsbury's gift certificate for your participation.
